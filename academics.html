Senior High School Programs

Our Senior High School tracks prepare students for both higher education and career opportunities. Available tracks:

  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)
  • Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)
  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)
  • Computer System Servicing (CSS)
  • Electical Intallation and Maintenance (EIM)

College Programs

Our college programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for professional careers. Offered courses:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor of Technical Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED)
  • Bachelor of Arts in English Language Studies (BAELS)
  • Associate in Computer Technology (ACT)
